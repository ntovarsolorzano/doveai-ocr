Why OCR Matters

Did you know that over 80% of business information is trapped in non-searchable documents and images? Optical Character Recognition (OCR) technology bridges this gap, making text in images and scanned documents accessible, searchable, and editable.

With an estimated 2.5 quintillion bytes of data created daily, OCR helps unlock valuable information from the 30% of digital content that exists as images or non-machine-readable formats.

DoveAI OCR leverages cutting-edge AI to accurately extract text while preserving document structure, formatting, and context.
